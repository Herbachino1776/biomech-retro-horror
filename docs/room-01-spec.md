# Room 01 Spec — Chamber of the Laughing Engine

## Purpose
This room is the first finished vertical slice space. It establishes the full visual and systemic identity of the project.

## Room Fantasy
The player enters a biomechanical ceremonial chamber built around an altar-like engine or idol. The architecture appears observant. The room feels like a shrine crossed with a slaughterhouse control room.

## Required Components
- layered parallax background
- stable floor platform geometry
- one lore trigger / narrated panel
- one locked or blocked progression point
- one combat space for standard enemies
- one miniboss arena zone

## Suggested Flow
1. Intro entry from left side
2. Environmental narration trigger
3. First skitter encounter
4. Mid-room hazard or obstacle
5. Husk sentinel encounter
6. Door/organ gate or seal
7. Miniboss activation
8. Exit or end-of-slice screen

## Visual Notes
- central idol/engine should dominate the chamber
- embedded eye motifs or face motifs are welcome
- floor and wall materials should imply flesh-metal fusion
- foreground pipes/cables can add depth but must not block player readability

## Audio Notes
- low oppressive ambient loop
- subtle mechanical wetness / pressure sounds
- impact sounds should feel dense and industrial-organic

## Acceptance Criteria
The room should be fully playable from start to end and visually coherent even if some art remains placeholder.
