# Game Design Doc

## Genre
Retro horror action-exploration with cinematic room presentation.

## Camera
2D side-view for the first vertical slice.

## Core Loop
1. Enter chamber
2. Observe environmental composition
3. Trigger text/lore or encounter
4. Fight or solve a simple traversal/puzzle obstacle
5. Advance deeper

## Player Abilities for Vertical Slice
- move left/right
- jump or short traversal action
- one primary attack
- take damage
- die and restart
- interact with terminals / lore triggers

## Enemy Archetypes
### 1. Skitter Servitor
Small fast biomechanical creature.
- patrols
- lunges at close range
- low health
- teaches spacing

### 2. Husk Sentinel
Medium humanoid/construct hybrid.
- slow approach
- deliberate attack windup
- higher health
- teaches timing and commitment

### 3. Miniboss: Laughing Engine
Stationary or semi-mobile grotesque altar-entity that attacks via emitted hazards, tendrils, or spawned minions.

## Pacing
Combat should be tense and readable, not swarm-heavy. Empty space and dread matter.

## Narrative Delivery
Use short environmental narration boxes, terminal entries, and chamber descriptions. Avoid overlong exposition during active danger.
