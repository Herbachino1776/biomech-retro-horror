# Chamber 03 Master Plan

Status: planning-only. **Do not treat this document as implementation proof.**

## Chamber 03 Identity and Purpose
Chamber 03 is the planned **Ossuary Choir Hall** and the first full sector-finale chamber of the game.

Its purpose is to:
- deliver the largest and most consequential chamber in the current bucket,
- pay off the Chamber 01 -> Chamber 02 escalation with a longer, encounter-heavy progression space,
- introduce the game's **first true boss** rather than another regular encounter or miniboss refinement,
- close the first major world thread: **dead-god palace / laughing-engine / subterranean biomech necropolis**.

Chamber 03 is ambitious by design, but that ambition must be landed through milestone-safe slices rather than one oversized pass.

## Ossuary Choir Hall Doctrine
Chamber 03 must preserve a clear identity: **Ossuary Choir Hall**.

Meaning in practice:
- the space should feel ceremonial, monumental, and terminal rather than transitional,
- bone, rib, choir-stall, reliquary, and ritual-threshold language should dominate the chamber identity,
- pacing should feel processional and oppressive, not arcade-chaotic,
- encounter placement should support the sense that the player is crossing an inhabited ritual hall on the way to judgment.

This identity should guide art, lore, pacing, encounter framing, and boss staging without requiring all of those layers to ship at once.

## Bucket 01 Role: Why Chamber 03 Is the Sector Finale
Bucket 01 currently carries the game's opening macro-thread:
- dead-god palace,
- laughing-engine ritual machinery,
- subterranean biomech necropolis descent.

Chamber 03 is the **sector finale** for that bucket because it is where that thread first reaches full consequence.

Finale role expectations:
- longer traversal than the prior chambers,
- heavier encounter density than the prior chambers,
- a stronger threshold/lore/payoff structure than prior chambers,
- a climactic entity that resolves the current bucket instead of merely extending it.

The chamber should feel like the end of a chapter, not simply Chamber 02.5.

## First True Boss Role and Expectations
Chamber 03 contains the **first true boss** of the game.

That means the climax entity should eventually provide:
- stronger presence and anticipation than Chamber 01's miniboss or Chamber 02's elite gate encounters,
- clearer arena framing and consequence,
- meaningful death payoff that reads as a sector-ending event,
- progression into the next area bucket after defeat.

The boss does not need every final layer in the first implementation pass, but Chamber 03 planning must protect that end-state from being diluted into a normal encounter sequence.

## All-Future-Chambers-at-Chamber-03-Scale Doctrine
Chamber 03 is not only a content target; it is the planning benchmark for future chambers.

Doctrine going forward:
- future chambers should aim for **Chamber-03-level consequence, length, and complexity**,
- every chamber should include substantial encounter design plus a climax entity,
- that climax entity may be a **miniboss**, an **elite/high-level enemy**, or a **true boss** depending on chamber role,
- chambers should differentiate themselves through art/theme, trigger placement, pacing, enemy placement, and climax tier rather than by shrinking ambition.

This does **not** mean every future chamber should be implemented in one giant pass. It means their design stakes should remain high even when implementation is chunked.

## Chamber 02 -> Chamber 03 Transition Rule
The Chamber 02 -> Chamber 03 handoff must be a real progression transition.

Rule:
- Chamber 02 should transition into Chamber 03 through an **actual gate / progression object / threshold interaction**,
- do **not** rely on a fragile end-of-chamber lore-screen handoff as the primary continuity mechanism.

Lore can still support the transition, but the player's progression contract should be anchored by a stable in-world threshold object.

## Long-Term Target vs Single-Pass Scope
Long-term Chamber 03 targets include:
- stronger boss ambience and encounter identity,
- stronger sector-finale payoff,
- boss-death progression into the next area bucket.

These are required parts of the **full Chamber 03 vision**, but they do **not** all need to land in one implementation pass.

Planning rule:
- preserve the full target,
- ship it in slices,
- do not mistake partial milestone delivery for permission to reduce Chamber 03 into a small filler chamber.
